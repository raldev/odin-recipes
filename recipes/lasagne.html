Lasagne

Description:

This is a dish called "Lasagne". It is contains tomatoes, beef mince, pork mince, cheese and lasagne pasta sheets.

It is well tasty!

Ingredients:

  • 9 x Lasagne Pasta Sheets
  • 400g Pork Mince
  • 400g Beef Mince
  • 200g Mozarella Cheese
  • 200g Parmesan Cheese
  • 2 x Fresh Garlic Clove
  • 2 x Tbsp Tomato Puree
  • 1 x Tin of Chopped Tomatoes
  • 1 x Jar of Bechemel Sauce

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 180 degrees C
  2. Finely chop the garlic clove and cook in a medium sized frying pan over a medium-high heat
  3. Once the garlic has started to brown, add both types of mince and cook until browned
  4. Add both the tomato puree and chopped tomatoes to the pan, simmer for 20 mins at a medium heat
  5. Add 1/3 of the ragu to a 15cm x 30cm baking dish
  6. Lay 3 of the lasagne sheets over the ragu to cover it
  7. Coat the pasta sheets with a layer of 1/3 of the jar of bechemel sauce
  8. Add another layer of the ragu, then 3 more lasange sheets, then the sauce
  9. Repeat once more until you have 3 total layers
  10. Grate both cheese and mix together in a mixing bowl
  11. Cover the dish in the grated cheese mixture
  12. Bake in the oven for 40 mins at 180 degrees C
  13. Remove from the oven, allow 5 mins to cool
  14. Eat, enjoy and get fat!
