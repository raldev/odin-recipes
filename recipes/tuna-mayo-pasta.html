Tuna Mayo Pasta

Description:

Easiest

Dinner

Ever

Ingredients:

  • Can of Tuna
  • 75g Fusili Pasta (Dry Weight)
  • 2 x tbsp Mayonnaise
  • Pinch of Salt & Pepper
  • Tin of Sweetcorn

Steps:

  1. Boil water in a pan and add 1 tsp of salt
  2. Add the past to the pan and cook for as long as the packet insturcts
  3. Drain the tuna and the sweetcorn, mix with the mayo, salt and pepper
  4. Drain the cooked pasta and mix altogether
  5. Munch!
